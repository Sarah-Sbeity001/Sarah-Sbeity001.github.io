About

I'm a data and business analyst who works where data engineering meets business strategy — building the pipelines and dashboards, then reading the story the numbers tell. At StratHaus, I design interactive Power BI dashboards, analyze complex financial datasets in Excel, and support M&A valuation by benchmarking peer companies and assessing deal multiples.

My background spans the modern data stack: ETL/ELT with Talend, SSIS, and Azure Data Factory, data warehousing and OLAP cubes, and data governance with Microsoft Purview — foundations I built as a data engineer at Thales Middle East. I turn these into Power BI reports and Excel models that decision-makers actually use.

I care about making data trustworthy and useful: clean data models, clear KPIs, and analysis aligned to real business objectives. I hold a Computer & Communication Engineering diploma from the Lebanese University.
